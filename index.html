Finance Club at Georgetown MSB

The Finance Club assists students as they prepare for careers in financial services.

We do this by focusing on three main initiatives:

  • Sponsoring mulitple professional deveopment sessions with the same people who train Wall Street professionals
  • Holding panel discussions with second year students and alumni to help students better understand finance careers
  • Providing networking opportunities with alumni and other profesionals through events like Finance Day and Wall Street Week

Careers

The Finance Club supports students pursuing a diverse array of finance careers.

  • Investment Banking: Providing advice and services to help organizations with Mergers & Acquisitions, financing, and other transactions.
  • Corporate Finance: Budgeting, financial planning, analysis, and project finance as part of the finance team at a specific company.
  • Wealth Management: Advsing individuals, families, and foundations to develop wealth and investment management strategies.

Prospective Students

If you are interested in pursuing finance careers at MSB, we have numerous resources available to you.

Peer Advisors: Peer Advisors are available for one-on-one sessions for career exploration, resume reviews, mock interviews, and more.

Financial Modeling: The Finance Club provides two trainings (one in the Fall, one in the Spring) through Wall St. Prep, the same company who provide training at top investment banks, private equity firms and Fortune 1000 companies.

Finance Day: The official kickoff for recruiting where we bring in finance professionals across industries for a day of panel discussions, breakout sessions, and networking that gives students their first taste of what to expect during the fall.

Wall Street Week: Our week long trek to NYC where students have the opportunity to visit more than a dozen firms and set up coffee chats with professionals.
