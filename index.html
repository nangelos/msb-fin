Finance Club at Georgetown MSB

The Finance Club assists students as they prepare for careers in financial services.

We do this by focusing on xx main initiatives:

  • Sponsoring mulitple professional deveopment sessions with the same people who train Wall Street professionals
  • Holding panel discussions with second year students and alumni to help students better understand finance careers
  • Providing networking opportunities with alumni and other profesionals through events like Finance Day and Wall Street Week

The Finance Club supports students pursuing a diverse array of finance careers.

  • Investment Banking
  • Corporate Finance
  • Wealth Management
